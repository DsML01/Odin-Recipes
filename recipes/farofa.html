Farofa recipe

Brazilian toasted cassava flour

This pizza has the perfect textural contrast between the flavorful, crunchy crust and the soft, cheesy topping.

  • Cassava flour
  • Butter
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Chives

Now, follow these steps

  1. In a cold skillet, cook the bacon over medium heat until golden brown. The reason for that is because you need to render the bacon fat before frying it.
  2. Once the fat is rendered, add the butter and cook the bacon until golden brown.
  3. Next, add the garlic and sauté it for a minute
  4. Add the cassava flour and stir continuously to avoid letting it burn.
  5. once the farofa is nicely and evenly toasted, remove it from the heat. It shouldn’t take more than 5 minutes after adding the cassava flour.
  6. Now, add the parsley and chives and mix well the ingredients.
  7. And finally, put your farofa on a serving plate and bom apetite!
